New and Noteworthy 406

There's a new version of Add Share Do out today, build number 406.  If you hit refresh in your browser you'll see these features:

  • Altered how we add tasks. It should be quicker and more reliable. 
  • Sharing & editing drop downs no longer require you to click the button to share or edit a tag, just hit return.
  • Updated how sharing information is displayed.
  • You can now revoke share request before they are accepted.     
  • Tweaks to the print style sheet.
  • Updated Tour/Help with changes to Sharing and to make language across the site consistent. 
  • Database changes to help us introduce an exciting feature soon. 
  • Updated email addresses we sent from help email from.
  • Bug fixes.
