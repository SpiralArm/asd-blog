New and Noteworthy 355

The latest release contains:

  • managing groups and tags has had a facelift.
  • the bookmarklet now records the title of the page you're adding.  To get this feature, you need to remove your existing bookmarket (often just a matter of right-click and selecting delete), then add in the new version from the ASD preferences page.
  • add human.txt, see http://humanstxt.org/ for more information.
  • links to Intranet pages (or other private networks, such as http://localhost/) are now detected and appear in the links button to the left of the task.
  • various bug fixes.

You can upgrade to this verison by reloading the page in your web browser.
