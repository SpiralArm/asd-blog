What's new in 430

Release 430 of Add Share Do contains three bug fixes.  Please refresh ASD in your browser to upgrade: 

  • When viewing your groups, we sometimes didn't summarise the tags in the group.  We do now.
  • When deleting a tag, we now remove it from any groups you've set up. 
  • Entering :s and no word after was producing an error.  It will now search for all your tasks, and will take longer than we'd like it to.
  • It was not possible to change the order of tags.  Fixed, and you can again now.

Thank you for trying ASD and reporting these problems.
