Introducing Add Share Do

Quietly, a little before the end of the year, we made AddShareDo.com live. We were not happy with the balance of simplicity and sharing we found in other applications, so we built what we needed.  You can read more on the background on the about page.

What we think is great about the app is the simplicity...

  • add tasks by typing them into the big box at the top of the screen.  
  • organize tasks with #tags (#today, #tomorrow, #home, whatever you like)
  • sort to-dos by dragging on screen 
  • share at the #tag level
  • email in to-dos to your secret AddShareDo email address 
  • group tags to give you a view of the key tags you need 

We have quite a list of improvements already, but it's been our only day-to-day to-do app for a while now.

We're not making any big announcements at the moment, but feel free to try it out (there's no charging yet), and leave your comments here.
