Easier to share

We've made it easier to share a tag and to see who you're sharing with.  

Take a look at your tags in Add Share Do.  Next to the tag name you'll see two links: Edit and Share.  If you click Share you'll see an area appear that just contains the sharing controls.  Click Share again to close the window.

If you add a new address in the Sharing window, it instantly updates and allows you to add more.  We think this is a big improvement over the previous screen.

In case you're curious, the Edit button allows you to rename and delete a tag (or leave a tag someone else has shared with you).
