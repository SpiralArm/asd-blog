Three Lists of Three

PersonaDev, a "productivity and personal development blog for the tech savvy", has posted an idea for to-do lists that "sometimes get out of control, growing to gorilla-sized proportions":

Make three lists each day as you sit down to work. The first list is for 3 things that you are going to get done today. The second list is for 3 things that you will get done today if you have time. The third list is for 3 things that will get done eventually.

Not one I practice, but it might work for you.

In AddShareDo you could have three tags (perhaps: #do #maybe #eventually) and optionally create a group called "three" or "focus" to easily drill down to just those three tags.
