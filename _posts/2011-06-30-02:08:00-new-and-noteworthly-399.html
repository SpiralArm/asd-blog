New and Noteworthly 399

 There's a new version of Add Share Do out today, build number 399.  If you hit refresh in your browser you'll see these features:

  • Altered how we add tasks. It should be quicker and more reliable.
  • Updated how sharing information is displayed. 
  • You can now revoke a share request before a collaborator has accepted it. Useful if you mistype an email address or change your mind.
  • Tweaked how ASD looks when printed.
  • Updated Tour page with new sharing information.
  • Bug fixes.
