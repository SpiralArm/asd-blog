New and Noteworthy 382

There's a new version of Add Share Do out today, build number 382.  If you hit refresh in your browser you'll see these features:

  • We've made a big improvement to how to edit and share tags.  More about this in a separate post, but it means you can click "edit" next to a tag to rename it, and "share" to get a simplified view of who you're sharing and a single place where you can add more people to your tag.
  • Search has been improved so that the results now show completed tasks, and they're interactive, so you can uncheck them if you want.
  • There are now more ways to give us feedback: on the bottom of every page, on the help page and on the preferences page.  Let us know what you like, what you don't like.
  • If you're sorting tasks with someone else, the tasks now reorder on the other person's screen in real time.
  • When you're creating or changing a group of tags (Preferences > Tags and Groups), the tags you pick from are now ordered alphabetically which makes them easier to find.

The biggest change is that ASD is now a subscription service.  It's amazing value (it works out at 84p a month) but you get to try it with a 30 days fully-functioning trial.  No credit card needed: give it a go.
