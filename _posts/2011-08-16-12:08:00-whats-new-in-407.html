What's new in 407

See the full gallery on Posterous
Build 407 of Add Share Do is out.  Hit refresh in your browser to upgrade to the new features....

  • Improved shared editing: if someone else is editing a task you're looking at, you'll now see a small lock icon next to the task.  It stays there until they've finished editing the task.
  • Quick new group creation: you can group tags into groups, and to make it easier, we've now added a "New group..." action from the "Viewing" drop-down.  Select it to be taken to the page that lets you created and edit groups.
  • Tag suggestions: as you type, we now suggest tags you might want to add to your task.  Click the suggsested tag to add it to your task.  There'll be a separate blog post on this feature, but the more tasks you add, the better the suggestions get.  You need at least 40 tasks for suggestions to appear, and we don't suggest for tags that haven't been used at least 5 times.

There are also smaller improvements:

  • Better browser support: IE8, Chrome, Opera, Safari and all working fine with ASD.
  • Improved sign up form and login forms.
  • ...and the usual smattering of general bug fixes.

Let us know how you get on with these features.
