Rolling Lists

Lifehack has a nice article: Hate Your To-Do List? Try A Rolling List Instead.

The core steps are to list the steps, and prioritise them, and tackle them in order.  You can do this with Add Share Do: no need to fight "priority numbers" or "importance", just use the drag handle (=) on the right of every todo and drag a todo to where it should be on your list.  Simple.
