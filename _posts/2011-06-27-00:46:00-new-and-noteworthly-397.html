New and Noteworthly 397

  There's a new version of Add Share Do out today, build number 397.  If you hit refresh in your browser you'll see these features:
  • Split the daily activity email into added, completed and updated tasks to provide better insight into what has changed in your tasks.
  • We've made some small improvements to the appearance of the site and the printed appearance of your tasks.
  • We've fixed a long standing annoyance that if you followed a link to a specific page, but weren't logged in, we'd ask you to login but then show you your tasks regardless of what page you wanted.  We'd now take you to the page you originally asked for after login. 
  • Bug fixes.
