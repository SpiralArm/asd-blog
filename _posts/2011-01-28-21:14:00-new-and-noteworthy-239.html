New and Noteworthy 239

Tonights update to AddShareDo.com is a bug fix release, but this includes a couple of annoyances you may have noticed:

  • Tasks emailed in to your secret ASD email address were not appearing in the Activity section.  They are now.
  • When a task contains web addresses, ASD shows a menu to give you quick access to those links.  Clicking complete didn't close the menu.  It does now, and indeed clicking anywhere on the page will close any open link menus.

And that's release number 239 (the number is on the bottom of every page).
