Last 5 minutes of your day

The Harvard Business Review has a blog entry on The Best Way to Use the Last Five Minutes of Your Day.  Here's the proposition: "Every day, before leaving the office, save a few minutes to think about what just happened. Look at your calendar and compare what actually happened — the meetings you attended, the work you got done, the conversations you had, the people with whom you interacted, even the breaks you took — with your plan for what you wanted to have happen."

Add Share Do can help you do this in a couple of ways.  First, click that big Activity button at the top of the screen.  That will list what you did under a handy "Today" heading, and who helped you.

A slight variation is the daily email feature we've added.  Every day at 6am, it sends you an email of recent activity: what you added, what you completed, what your colleagues did.  It's not going to be for everyone (you can turn it off on the preferences page), but it might just be the moment of reflection you need.
