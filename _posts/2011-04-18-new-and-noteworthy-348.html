New and Noteworthy 348

See the full gallery on Posterous

Version 348 of ASD contains changes for emailing in tasks, tasks that contain links, and groups and tags.  Here are the details:

  • In the drop down list of tags and groups you're viewing, "All Tags" now appears under "groups" rather than "tags".  We think this makes more sense.
  • When you change the tag or group you're viewing, we now remember your last selection. When you login back in to ASD, or if you login on a different browser, on the iPhone or the iPad, we take you to your last selected group or tag.
  • Talking of which, if you do login on an iPhone or iPad, the email address login field is a little easier to use now.
  • As you know, if you have a task that contains links, we offer a drop down menu giving you access to those links.  As of 348, if you have a task that contains just one link, the icon changes and it takes to you that link with one click.
  • We've seen a couple of emailed-in tasks sent to the wrong address.  We now send back a warning email if this happens.
  • On the subject of emailed-in tasks, on the preferences page you can now download your secret email in address in a format to add to your adddress book (we've tried it with Google Contacts and the Mac Address Book so far).

Finally, we're thinking about renaming "tags" to "labels".   What do you think?
